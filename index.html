Reservation Priority (Vorbehalt) and Bonus Points

Determining What is Played
If one or more players announce a reservation, the priority is:
  1. Misdeal / Throwing In (highest priority): A player can declare a misdeal before the first card is played if they have:
    • Less than 3 trump cards
    • 5 or more Kings
    • 7 or more Aces/Tens (Volle)
    • All personal trumps are lower than the Jack of Diamonds
  2. Hochzeit (Wedding): Outranks any Solo.
  3. Solo
